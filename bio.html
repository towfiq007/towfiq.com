My Journey

Hello! I'm Md. Towfiqur Rahman, a passionate technology professional with over 8 years of experience in software development and cybersecurity. My journey in technology began with a deep curiosity about how things work, which led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the prestigious Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects, from developing enterprise-grade security solutions to leading high-performing engineering teams. My work has taken me through various domains, including cybersecurity, cloud computing, and software architecture, allowing me to develop a well-rounded perspective on technology and its applications.

What drives me is the opportunity to solve complex problems and create solutions that make a real impact. I'm particularly passionate about cybersecurity and the critical role it plays in our increasingly digital world.
